What is the difference between custom App Development and web builders?

Custom App Development involves writing tailored, high-performance code specific to your business needs, offering complete flexibility, scalability, and security. Unlike standard template builders, it runs faster, features unique UI/UX, and can be optimized specifically for SEO crawlers and customer retention.

How long does it take to deploy a custom application and see SEO results?

A typical custom application takes 4 to 8 weeks to design, develop, and launch. SEO results, such as organic traffic and higher search rankings, generally begin showing indexable progress within 3 to 6 months after launching optimized content structures.

Will redesigning my web page impact my existing site ranking history?

No, we perform complete mapping of all site links and setup 301 redirects for modified URLs. Our development process guarantees that current ranking authority carries over smoothly to your new, responsive custom application.
